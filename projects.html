Our Process

From first chat to aftercare — a smooth, transparent journey.

1. Initial Consultation

We begin with a detailed meeting to fully understand your lifestyle, goals, and vision. This crucial step ensures the entire project is custom-tailored to your unique story and requirements.

2. Site Documentation & Analysis

We meticulously document every aspect of the space and analyze your style preferences to create a comprehensive design brief that guides the project from start to finish.

3. Design & Planning Phase

We transform your ideas into thoughtful concepts—layouts, mood boards, and design plans—iterating with your feedback so the design reflects your story and functional needs.

4. Material & Finishes Selection

Collaborate with our experts to select high-quality materials and finishes that align with your aesthetic—ensuring both lasting durability and an elegant, cohesive look.

5. Feasibility Check

We review the design and selections to confirm practicality, structural soundness, and budget alignment—paving a smooth path from concept to reality.

6. Customization & Engineering

We engineer structural modifications and bespoke elements—including modular units, built-ins, and fixtures—custom-fit to your space.

7. On-Site Non-Modular Work

We begin the on-site civil & structural work—demolition, electrical, plumbing, and wall alterations—building a strong foundation for later stages.

8. Installation of Modular Units

We install all pre-fabricated modular units—kitchens, wardrobes, and storage systems—with precision for a seamless fit.

9. On-Site Customization

We fine-tune on site—modifications and adjustments to furniture and fixtures—so every element integrates perfectly with your space.

10. SNAG Clearance & Site Finalization

We conduct a meticulous final inspection and resolve minor snags. The site is cleaned and cleared—ready for the reveal.

11. The Grand Reveal & Handover

We walk you through the finished space and ensure every detail meets your expectations before the official handover.

12. Maintenance & Aftercare

We provide guidance on caring for your new interiors and remain available for any post-installation support.

At Neshstories, we believe every space should embody the essence of its inhabitants. “Create the Story You Love, with Neshstories”
